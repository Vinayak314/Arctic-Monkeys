A concept album with a space-age, lounge pop aesthetic, Tranquility Base Hotel & Casino is a piano-driven record focusing on ambiance and lyrical storytelling. It tackles themes of dystopia, technology, and existential musings, featuring tracks like "Four Out of Five" and "Star Treatment."
