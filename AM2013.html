Summary

A commercial breakthrough for the band, AM fuses hip-hop beats, R&B influences, and rock. The album explores themes of late nights, desire, and introspection, with major hits such as "Do I Wanna Know?," "R U Mine?," and "Why'd You Only Call Me When You're High?"
